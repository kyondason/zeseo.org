About

We as an organization (ZESEO) are focusing our energies in the field of the environment. As within Zanzibar, sustainable environment is an important dimension for our economy. Thus in order to make the economy thing sustainable then the same energy must be applied to the overall environment..

Mission

ZESEO is dedicated towards promoting, motivating and inciting environment conservation, awareness of the domestic tourism within our community and helping each other in transforming social and economic challenges of the individuals, families and community into opportunities.

Vission

We envision making Zanzibar an epitome of better verdant clean environment and sustainable ground for tourism.

Our Fields of Intervention

The Organisation dedicated her policies on ensuring environmental sustainability and a form of tourism practices that involve conserving environment and improving well-being of local people.

TOURISM SECTOR

Tourism as potential sector needs head to head monitoring for the purpose of reducing its adverse impacts on environment.

ENVIRONMENT SUSTAINABILITY

Environmental programs that will spearhead environmental conservation and increasing resillience on Climate Change stresses

CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

Community awareness and Education to young cluster concerning environmental movements is potential for environment sustainability

Ecosystem Restoration for People.
UNEP, FAO
Posted on: June 03, 2021

The world is facing severe challenges. Billions of people around the world are suffering the consequences of the climate emergency, food and water insecurity, and the COVID-19 pandemic. Ecosystems are an indispensable ally as we meet these challenges. Protecting them and managing their resources in a sustainable manner is essential. But just increasing the protection and sustainable management of our remaining natural landscapes and oceans will not be enough: the planet’s degraded ecosystems and the huge benefits that they provide must also be restored.

Healthy Oceans,Resilient World
UN Climate Change News
Posted on: June 08, 2021

World Oceans Day, celebrated annually on 8 June, is a reminder of the major role the oceans have in the health of people and the planet. While oceans have suffered heavy impacts of climate change, such as sea level rise, they are fast becoming recognized as a vital ally in achieving a decarbonized and resilient world.
